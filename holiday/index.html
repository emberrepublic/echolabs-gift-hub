For Armani

At 135–140cm she should not need the 92–122cm supervising-companion rule by height. Adults may still choose to ride together for confidence, queues and fun.

Five-hour priority plan

  1. Start Minion Land: Despicable Me Minion Mayhem, Silly Swirly, Buggie Boogie.
  2. Move to Far Far Away: Puss In Boots, Enchanted Airways, skip Magic Potion Spin unless queue is short.
  3. The Lost World: Canopy Flyer, Jurassic Park Rapids if you are prepared to get wet.
  4. Sci‑Fi City: TRANSFORMERS. Battlestar only if Armani actively wants the big coaster.
  5. Finish Ancient Egypt/New York: Treasure Hunters or Sesame Street as lower-stress fillers; Mummy only if she wants scary/dark thrill.

Skip / conditional

  • Likely skip if time is tight: Magic Potion Spin, Dino‑Soarin’, Treasure Hunters, Sesame Street — good, but lower value for a 135–140cm child unless queues are tiny.
  • Conditional on courage: Battlestar Galactica and Revenge of the Mummy.
  • Conditional on motion sensitivity: Minion Mayhem, TRANSFORMERS, Accelerator.
  • Conditional on wet clothes: Jurassic Park Rapids Adventure.
